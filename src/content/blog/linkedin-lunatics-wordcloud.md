Fine. You want me to use AI. I'll at least use it to make me laugh, roll my eyes, or find ways to cut through the garbage. That being said, I had ChatGPT scrape the LinkedIn posts that were shared/flamed/roasted in the <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/LinkedInLunatics/">r/LinkedInLunatics</a> subreddit in the last year to see what words were used the most.

Hilariously, the top words were:
- Hustle
- Founder
- Leadership
- CEO
- Mindset

Ironically, "authentic" is in there as well.

Of course, these were just the posts that caused enough of a reaction that someone screenshot it and posted it on a completely separate site, but we can agree that LinkedIn vernacular has veered away from natural human language.

So how will I use this? When everyone has access to tools that shape our narratives, I want to know what NOT to say to stand out more. So don't be like this word cloud and use a thesaurus.
