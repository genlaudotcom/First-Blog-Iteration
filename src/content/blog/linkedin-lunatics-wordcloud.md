Fine. You want me to use AI. I'll at least use it to make me laugh, roll my eyes, or find ways to cut through the garbage. That being said, I had ChatGPT scrape the LinkedIn posts that were shared/flamed/roasted in this subreddit in the last year to see what words were used the most.

Hilariously, the top words were:
- Hustle
- Founder
- Leadership
- CEO
- Mindset

Ironically, "authentic" is in there as well.

So how will I use this? When everyone has access to tools that shape our narratives, I want to know what NOT to say to stand out more. So don't be like this word cloud and use a thesaurus. PLEASE.
